A New Croatian Translation of the Stabat Mater

12-11-2024

We are excited to announce a new addition to our website – a Croatian translation of the original Latin Stabat Mater text! Mr. Leopold, a young classical music enthusiast from Croatia, alerted us to this translation. Thank you very much! The Croation translation is number 33 in the list of languages!

We extend our heartfelt thanks to Mr. Leopold for bringing this translation to our attention. Additions like this enable us to continuously expand and improve the Stabat Mater Website, so that lovers of this music all over the world can discover and share in this rich musical heritage.

Do you also have an interesting find or suggestion for our website? Let us know through our new Stabat Mater forum! We look forward to your contribution.
